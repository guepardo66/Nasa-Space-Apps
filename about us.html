About Us

Today, global climate change is accelerating and significantly affecting our planet. It has been noted that the impact of mineral dust is particularly important at the regional level. EMIT is a sensor sent by NASA to provide more detailed information on these issues. Although EMIT's data, which could contribute to a better understanding of global warming, is publicly available, very few people are aware of it. This is where Emeet comes in. Through our website, which makes it much easier to explain and learn from the data obtained from EMIT, you can easily get acquainted with this magical machine!
